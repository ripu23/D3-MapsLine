Understanding the trends in world population.

The figure on the left shows the population of different countries through color encoding.

As can be seen China and India occupies the top position with intense red color.

Hovering over any country displays the country name and population in millions.

Clicking over any country plots a new line chart with population from 1995 to 2017.

The visualization can also be zoomed and dragged.

Both of the visualization are made using D3.js

The figure on the left shows the population trends from the year 1995 to 2017.

Most of the countries shows the linear pattern with year being directly proportional to the population.

Germany & Russia however show a different kind of relationship between year and population. Both of them show quite a wavy pattern.
